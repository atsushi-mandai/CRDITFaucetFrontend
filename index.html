What is CRDIT Faucet?

This website interacts with the CRDIT Faucet contract and provides a front end for receiving the ERC20 token "CRDIT" issued on the BNB chain for free.

The CRDIT Faucet contract is assigned the right to newly issue CRDIT from the CRDIT contract up to 100,000 CRDIT, and free distribution is performed using the issuance limit.

After connecting the wallet, the user chooses a fixed amount or a random amount to receive CRDIT. If the user chooses to receive a flat-rate CRDIT, they will receive 95 CRDIT. If the user chooses to receive a random amount of CRDIT, there is a one-third chance that they will receive a CRDIT that is 0.8x, 1x, or 1.2x multiplied by 95. These are the figures as of July 2022.

After receiving a CRDIT once, the user must wait 24 hours before receiving the next CRDIT.
